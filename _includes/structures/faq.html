Why would I need a private tutorial?
Private tutorials are mainly for those that don't want their stuff accessible by the world of the internet. All premium tutorials have full privacy controls. Only authorized people (those that you invite) will be allowed to view your tutorial.

Can I host my premium tutorial on my own server or company website?
But of course you can. As a premium subscriber you can download the HTML zip package of your tutorial and host it on your own server/website (as per our terms of service this is a single server license). The HTML zip package is optimized and accessible by mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers.

What else do I get?
You will be able to publish your tutorial as step by step word doc or powerpoint. If you rollover docx and pptx you will see a quick shot of what this looks like.

How can I cancel and what happens to my premium tutorials?
If you cancel your account, all tutorials both public and private will be deleted immediately. You can go to your profile dropdown and select My Account and Delete Account. It's the last option.

Should I choose the yearly option?
It's up to you. If you do decide to pay for the year up front, you'll get two months free.

What happens if I stop paying?
Your account will remain active and you can continue making public tutorials for free like usual but all your premium tutorials will be locked from editing. To edit or download the files again, you'll have to catch up on your past due amount.
